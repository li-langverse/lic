## Summary

Ships the httpd P0 wire-buffer and trusted-network prerequisites in `lic`:

- **`std/bytes`:** `bytes_len`, `bytes_slice`, `bytes_append`, `bytes_byte_at`, `bytes_push_byte`, `stringview_*`, and **Reader/Writer** incremental APIs.
- **`raises Net`:** effect propagation enforced on `std/runtime/seam.li` externs; policy tests in `li-tests/effects/` and `li-tests/net_trusted/`.
- **Trusted syscall RFC:** [2026-05-16-li-trusted-net-rfc.md](../superpowers/specs/2026-05-16-li-trusted-net-rfc.md) accepted; v1 axioms in `docs/semantics/trusted.lean`.
